The idea

Two obsessions under one roof.

Static & Stems started as an argument between a coffee roaster and a record collector about which deserved more patience. Neither won, so the shop does both properly: beans roasted in small batches on-site, and a listening room built around a single rule — whatever's on the platter plays start to finish.

Weekday mornings, it's a quiet roastery counter. Weekend evenings, it becomes a proper listening bar.
